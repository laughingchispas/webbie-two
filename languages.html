linguistic training

Only in very rare cases is it possible to learn a foreign language to a truly high level without 'bathing ourselves' in that language (i.e. living abroad). My natural love for foreign language, culture and music has led me to live in the south of Spain, the French Riviera, Buenos Aires, Barcelona, northern Italy and now Brussels. For me, this the truly crucial piece to knowing any language from inside and out and is perhaps my biggest strength as a linguist.

the communicative method

All my language classes are based on the communicative method of language acquisition whereby students are immersed in situations that mirror real-life situations and therefore provide the students with the tools to use the English they have learned in class out in the world.
